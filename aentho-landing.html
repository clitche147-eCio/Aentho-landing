Aentho was born from frustration. We watched too many hardworking business owners lose money, lose records, and feel stressed about something that should be simple.

We spent months talking to shop owners, food sellers, tavern owners, and traders. We asked one question: "What's the biggest problem tracking your business?"

The answers were always the same — lost notes, unknown profit, and no time to learn complicated software. So we built Aentho: the simplest, most powerful business tracker for everyday businesses.
